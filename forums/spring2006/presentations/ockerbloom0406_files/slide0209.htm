Very few serials completely copyrighted after 1922
•WorldCat lists over 200,000 serials with significant dates between 1923 and 1950
–May undercount based on journals not starting or ending in that range
–May overcount based on duplicate entries for some serials
•We found only ~1300 serials that renewed any issue copyrights during that time
•Most significant serials publishing then did not renew all (or any) of their issues
–Weak correspondence between extent of renewal and significance to researchers
