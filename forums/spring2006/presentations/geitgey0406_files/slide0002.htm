Why develop a POD program?
lCreation of MOA spurred library interest in exploring ways to multipurpose the collections’ public domain digital volumes
lMOA users periodically requested to buy printed copies of the works
lInitial thought was to sell reprints to fund more digitization and produce replacement copies for the physical collection
lPOD technologies were improving and becoming more prevalent
lSPO was officially charged with developing a POD program in 2001
