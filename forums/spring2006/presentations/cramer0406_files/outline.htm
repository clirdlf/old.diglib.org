SDR Context
  • Bit Level Preservation environment with expectations of offering (more) and differing levels of services
  • Have built and intend to facilitate an automated production environment for digitization & receipt of digital materials
  • Increasing chances of normalized content and metadata using a target manifest (TM) = core metadata, structure & file inventory  expressed as METS document
