n      AccrualPeriodicity
nElement Description: The frequency with which items are added to a collection.
n
nGuidelines for content creation:
nTerms from controlled vocabularies may be developed for the use of a particular project or in general use in a cultural materials context.
n
nExamples:  Annual, Irregular
n
n        AccrualPolicy
nElement Description: The policy governing the addition of items to a collection.
n
nExamples:  Active, Closed
n
