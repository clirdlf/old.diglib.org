Change in Copyright Law
  • Before 1986: duration based on date of copyright or creation
  • Prior 1989: notice required
  • After: duration based on life of the author
  • Now: life of the author plus 70 years

Why would libraries and users benefit from copyright metadata schema now?
  • Copyright controls “copying” and “copies” of creative expression
  • Every use in digital environment creates a “copy”
  • Every use potentially implicated or controlled by copyright
  • Libraries throughout time mission provide long-term access and use of collections
  • Copyright metadata schema designed to support use and uses of digital information
