Parting thoughts…
Profile places emphasis on “object modeling” and structural characteristics of the digital object
Whether one uses METS or another “wrapper” or “aggregator” (such as MPEG-21) one will still have to go through the object modeling process for complex objects
This approach requires a logical analysis of the program content of the content disc and that that analysis be reflected in the cataloging of the item (i.e. there is an emphasis on “embedded analytic records”). This is new and it is labor intensive but is has big payoffs in the subsequent usability of the object.
