Logical Structure
•If there is more than one work recorded on the compact disc, the MODS element must contain one relatedItem TYPE=constituent child element for each work. All relatedItem TYPE=constituent elements must be given an ID attribute and must have a titleInfo child element.
•
•If a work has logical sub-parts, those sub-parts may be represented with relatedItem TYPE=constituent child elements. Examples of logical sub-parts of a work are the movements of a musical work, the acts and scenes of a dramatic work, etc. The logical structure of each work will thus be represented as a hierarchy of relatedItems.
