Hemispheric Institute of Performance and Politics
The Hemispheric Institute Digital Video Library is currently a three year collaboration between NYU’s Digital Library Program a the Hemispheric Institute of Performance and Politics (HI), supported by a grant from the andrew .... mellon. The HI mission is to provide an open resource for those scholars, artists and activists working on the relation between politics and performance in the Americas.  To that end the Digital Library is digitizing and preserving 250 hours of video per year of original performances, lectures, and symposia.
