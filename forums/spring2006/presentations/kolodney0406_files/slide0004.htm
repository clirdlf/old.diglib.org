As Aaron mentioned, some of the keywords in the management of small and large scale projects in parallel, are consistency, structured workflows, and above all, efficiency.
Keeping naming schemes consistent is sometimes a challenge; On one hand, there is a need to cater to the clients’ wish for descriptive names; on the other hand, it is essential to reduce confusion between units and across the board. The use of acronyms, common shortcuts, and library world identifiers, facilitates the main goal of making the small projects as efficient as the large ones.
