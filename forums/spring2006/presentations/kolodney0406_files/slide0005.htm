•Large scale:
•Structured, streamlined, parallel processes
•Numerous parallel projects at the same time
•Batch processing – less quality, more efficiency à
•Low quality is acceptable, sampled QC, less time-consuming treatment (imaging/OCR/encoding)

In their nature, small scale projects consist of unique items or special collections, such as archival historical documents or fragile rare books – these items might demand special care and handling, full quality control, 100% OCR accuracy, and high encoding level. Processing would be linear and detailed.

In order to achieve high efficiency while performing large scale projects, one needs to compromise on quality, and at the same time, to streamline workflows; low or less-than-perfect quality is acceptable, and time-consuming processes are replaced with automated batch processes.
