Working Group Characteristics
  • Lightweight, quick output of recommendations/procedures for community review
  • Finish work in the next few months
  • Chairperson, working members, staff support from RLG
4
Description & Digitization Coordination Working Group
  • Set descriptive metadata guidelines for Open Content Alliance objects
  •  Deliverables:
    • document outlining what descriptive metadata will be made available to those interested in building services on top of the OCA aggregation
    • document outlining what metadata the OCA needs to make available for selection and analysis processes.
  • Chairs: Jim Michalko, RLG, Locan Dempsey, OCLC
  • RLG Staff Support: Günter Waibel
5
Digital Preservation Working Group
  • Recommend minimum requirements necessary to engender community-wide trust in the OCA collections as persistent, cultural, digital resources
  • Leverage extensive work already conducted by a variety of institutions
  • One-time meeting of key stakeholders (followed by a period of public review and comment)
  • Chair: Dan Greenstein, CDL
  • RLG Staff Support: Robin Dale
6
Contribution Working Group
  • Solicit contributions of digital materials, facilities, tools, and financial support from organizations under Open Content Alliance principles
  • Evaluate and prioritize contributions offered to the OCA
  • Expand and interpret the OCA principles
  • Recognizing contributions that warrant recognition
  • Chair: Rick Prelinger, OCA/Internet Archive
  • RLG Staff Support: Anne Van Camp
7
Book Formats Working Group
  • Document existing practice for significant legacy and parallel scanning projects
    • Take advantage of the work being done by other digitization projects and avoid duplication of effort
    • Help to inform what formats should be stored at the OCA
    • Identify tools needed to convert formats on ingest.
  • Deliverables: A document that analyzes other major digitization projects for: file format, text file format, structural metadata, OCR data
  • Chair: Stu Blair, Internet Archive
  • RLG Staff Support: Merrilee Proffitt
8
Scribe Scanning Workflow Group
  • Will describe the workflow for digitizing volumes using the Internet Archive's Scribe book scanners
  • Deliverables:
    • document that describes in detail the workflow involved for libraries and others who are having the Internet Archive capture and convert physical volumes from their collections.
    •  document describing minimum requirements to set up a Scribe implementation
  • Chair: Robin Chandler, CDL
  • RLG Staff Support: Ricky Erway
