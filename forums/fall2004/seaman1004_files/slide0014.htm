$24 million funding from the latest budget will allow New Zealand to ward off "digital amnesia", and protect New Zealand's documentary heritage for future generations.
"New Zealand, through this new innovation, will be an international leader in the field of preserving a nation's cultural heritage, a challenge that archives and libraries around the world are facing. This bold initiative of ours ensures that New Zealanders truly get to preserve their histories for future generations to come," says Penny Carnaby.
