Tools for Interpretation
"human interpretation is the heart of the humanities. . . . devising computer-assisted ways for humans to interpret more effectively vast arrays of the human enterprise is the major challenge. Contextual issues are part of that: time/age/period, theoretical model(s), topics, themes, preconditions for comprehension, helpers for comprehension, applications which use them, datasets associated with them, and so forth.”
Michael Jensen, Director of Publishing Technologies
National Academies Press
DC Meeting, April 27, 2004
