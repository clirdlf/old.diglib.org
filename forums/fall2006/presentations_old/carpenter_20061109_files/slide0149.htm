NutchWAX
nNutchWAX bundles Nutch/Hadoop with extensions for
nworking with archived content, including:
nAdapted Nutch fetcher step to go against archives rather than open net
nIndex-time and query-time plug-ins to allow querying of a records' location in a repository
nAwareness of capture-times and multiple captures per URL
nSending searchers/browsers to an associated Wayback Machine
n
nCurrent Release: v0.6.0
n
nNotable Additions & Changes Since Katrina
nMoved to a map/reduce version of Nutch in May 2006
nPlanned Enhancements
nImproved performance and stability for indexing jobs of 100+ million captures
