OCLC Programs & Research
Mass digitization is just one component of a larger programs and research focus on managing the collective collection.
In this area,
•We are shaping a community discussion on the principles and “best practices” of library partnerships in mass digitization – work that will be carried forth by the ARL Committee on Scholarly Communications
•We are looking at using data-mining strategies to inform selection for conversion and storage.
•We are examining the role of special collections in large-scale digitization
•We are giving new thought to the role of the Registry of Digital Masters – there are multiple competing efforts or aspirations that call for a re-evaluation of how well we are meeting community expectations.
•We are looking at shared print storage and the collaborative management of multi-institutional collections.
