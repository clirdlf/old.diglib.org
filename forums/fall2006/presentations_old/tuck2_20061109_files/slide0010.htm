Legal Deposit and Websites: Progress
nProposal being considered by LDAP as follows:
nRegulation to be put in place to enable legal deposit from the public domain web space of the UK through a combination of domain-level harvesting and selective web archiving
nRetention of such content by the Legal Deposit Libraries to be enabled
nAccess to the content within the Legal Deposit Libraries infrastructure to be enabled and not hindered by legal barriers such as FOI
nTechnical mechanisms to be put in place to make deposit easier and more effective
n
nAims:
nRegulation to come into force as soon as is practicable
nImpact assessment and consultation to be wide enough to include all relevant publisher groupings
n
nRealistic timescale:
nNot likely to be implemented before 2008
