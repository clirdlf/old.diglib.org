Working with Publishers
nAutomated Content Access Protocol (ACAP)
Led by Rightscom, aim of this pilot project, working with publisher bodies, is to:
nDevelop a specification which will allow the publisher of a website or any piece of content to attach extra data, in a  standardised form, to specify what uses of that piece of content or of the website are permissible
nBritish Library intends to participate in the pilot project in view of potential benefits in connection with legal deposit legislation
