VRA and CCO have developed extensive lists of the kinds of relationships that can exist between collections, works, and images. CCO has gone a step further and categorized the relationship types. We will learn more about how this works once a body of records is created using VRA 4.0 and CCO, and organizations begin exchanging them. In VRA 4, each level of description is an independent record, and these relation elements and types will drive system functionality to associate them for retrieval and display and exchange.
