Possible Approaches for Subject Browsing
§Parse or index list by subjects’ facets
q19th century - French - Posters
 French - Posters - 19th century
 Posters - French - 19th century
§Map subjects to selected thesauri and provide cross-references
§Build hierarchical browsing on top of some taxonomy
qPrinting & Graphics > Circus Posters
Culture & Society > Posters - French - 19th century
The first approach, though it doesn’t require any additional metadata, would’ve resulted in actually increasing our list-size since we would essentially be duplicating subjects according to their facets - which was the opposite of the goal of this project. And the last 2 approaches were going to require at least some new metadata or meta-metadata, to be produced we decided early on to try to see what we could do with the metadata we already had on hand.
