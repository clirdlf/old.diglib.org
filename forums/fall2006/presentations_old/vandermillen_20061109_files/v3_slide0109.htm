Features of VC
• Scheduled harvesting of metadata from
HOLLIS (OPAC), VIA (Visual Information
Access) and Harvard Geospatial Library
• Web-based search, browse, and display
interface; short and full record views
• Search results include thumbnails and links
to digital objects
• Flexible configuration
– “Out of the box” stand alone public interface
– “Embedded” option for integrating into web
sites, such as Harvard Open Collections
sites
