Illustrative example of the use of the tree to display a set of thumbnails.  In fact, the Ray Charles album does not contain a booklet (least of all a booklet on the tango), and these pages are shown for illustration only.  Since the term booklet has been selected on the tree--not an individual page, which may also be selected--the window at right displays an array of page thumbnails.
Navigation within the booklet is afforded by the tree and by the panel under the image reproduction.
