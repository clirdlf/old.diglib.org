A note on sustainability
• DLF seeks to incubate digital library
initiatives, not to own and operate them them
in perpetuity (it’s not a McD franchise)
• Sustainability is a more secure strategy than
digital “life support” (whether in the form of
speculative ongoing grant funding,
dependence upon ongoing institutional and
consortial investment, or ongoing
government subsidy)
• Sustainability requires the creation of value
and holds us to a high standard
