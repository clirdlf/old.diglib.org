Increasingly, analog content is produced digitally (though still distributed in analog form).

Relative mix of content will shift towards more “born digital”.

Java applets are an example of content with small granularity, but potentially very high reusability. (Granularity inversely proportional to reusability.)

Implication of decreasing granularity is that more users can become creators: reusing, repurposing content.

Also providing commentary on content will also be a contribution.

“Free” as in valued as a “public good” and hence supported publicly.
