Transformation to Persistent Object Form
• Characterize all significant properties of the things that are
to be preserved.
• Express these requirements in formal models
• Transform the objects by
– Encapsulating them in metadata defined in the models
– Eliminating other technical characteristics that are dependent on
specific hardware or software, proprietary, or subject to
obsolescence.
• Use software “mediators” to enable future technologies to
interpret the models and metadata
– to rebuild and repopulate collections
– support information discovery and delivery.
