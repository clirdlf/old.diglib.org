A P-book IS:
Based on UNESCO definition
Working definition of a p-book
–Printed on paper (excludes microform)
–Language material
–Monograph
–Physical description
–Form of item = regular or large print
–Title does not include a GMD
–Substantial length (> 49 pages; > 25 to include juvenile titles)
–Excludes manuscripts (dissertations and theses)
Language Material – byte 6 of Leader = a
Monograph – byte 7 of Leader = m
007 byte 0 = a, b, d
008 byte 23 Form of item = blank, d, or r
245 has no subfield h (GMD)
260 b (publisher) or 020
> 25 to include juvenile titles) – found several studies that included this pagination criterion for juvenile books and we are attempting to substantiate this
