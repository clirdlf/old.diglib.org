DLF Goal 7
•The establishment of an ongoing and comprehensive evaluation program to study:
o --how scholars and other researchers, students of all levels, and citizens everywhere make use of the digital library for research, learning, discovery, and collaboration;
o --how such usage compares with that of traditional libraries and other sources of information;
o --how digital libraries affect the mission, economics, staffing, and organization of libraries and other institutions; and
o --how to design systems to encourage access by individuals representing a broad spectrum of interests.
