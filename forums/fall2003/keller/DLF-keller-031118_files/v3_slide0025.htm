DODL working groups
• The first working group is charged with strategic
planning for content development (component 1),
both the pooling of existing content, and the
development of systemic collection development.
• The second working group will focus on the
technology and enabling infrastructure that will
support DODL and plan for its extension and
deepening of sharing (components 2 and 3).
•  Working together, the working groups will define
the finding system.
