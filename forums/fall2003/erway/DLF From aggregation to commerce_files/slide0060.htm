Trove Portal
The portal is the place to which RCM contributors can link from their sites to refer their users to their content in the context of other collections.
The portal is the place where users can search all Trove and only Trove content.
The portal is the place where we collect eyeball counts for potential sponsors
The portal is really just a pretty face for the Hub – perhaps a single HTML page (or a few describing the content)
The portal merely uses one of the Internet Search Engines with a limit to things in the Hub.
When users go to the Trove portal, they can be redirected to the subscription service if they are authorized.
