Internet Search Engines
The search engines are, for most users, the way users find Trove content.  They are also the primary means for marketing Trove to the general public.

From some services (e.g., Google images), users may be able to go directly to spokes.  From the rest, they’d be referred to the item in the Hub and from there make a selection.
Utilizing the existing internet search engines has these benefits:
Makes Trove available to users where they usually look
No need to publicize a separate site
No need to establish a separate search engine and interface.
