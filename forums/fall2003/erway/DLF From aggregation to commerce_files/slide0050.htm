We’re beginning with a negotiation with a stockhouse that is willing to take on all of our content in a non-exclusive arrangement, so that other stockhouses can represent Trove content, too.

RLG will export works as XML records and a concordance linking them to form the initial hub site.   Stockhouses will receive the data they require from the hub site.  RLG will optimize Hub content for Internet search engines spidering.

We’ve decided to start with a modest version of the hub model to allow us to experiment and prototype while we explore building it out with all the requisite relationships.
