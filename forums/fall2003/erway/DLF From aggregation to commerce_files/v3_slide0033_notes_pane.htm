The next phase of the Cultural Materials Initiative is to take what we’ve built for the academic community and repurpose it to meet a variety of goals:
--reaching new audiences,
--providing broader awareness of and access to the institutions' special collections,
--and testing the waters of commercial licensing.

There were several key areas of needed expertise that were outside of our normal practice, so it seemed a good candidate for some outsourcing.

The working name for this service is Trove.  The academic subscription service is hereafter referred to as RCM.
