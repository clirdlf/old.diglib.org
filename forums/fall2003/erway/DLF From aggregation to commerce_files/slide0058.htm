Hub
It’s important to have one place where all the Trove content is accessible in order to include all contributors.  No one visits the hub directly.  Rather they follow a link to a specific item.

The Hub will also be the supply depot for any partners who need to load our data in their own systems.

Optimizing for internet indexing ensures the widest possible audience and precludes the need to develop a separate search engine and interface.

Connecting at the item level to spoke services allows the users (whether consumer or professional) to choose from the services available for that particular item.

Redirecting to the Trove portal is important.  This is the way to search all Trove content and only Trove content.  And visitorship will encourage sponsorship.
