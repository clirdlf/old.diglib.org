Professional stock houses will want to select images to offer in their services.  Stock houses have established markets and will select images that they believe will appeal to their markets. Some images will be available through more than one stock house.

These selected images will likely be ingested into their systems in accordance with the rights/terms established by the Alliance.  We’ll receive a portion of the licensing fee to share directly with the contributor.

Most stock house clients will find our content through the stock house interface.
