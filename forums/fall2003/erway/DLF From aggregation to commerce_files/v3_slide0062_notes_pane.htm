Redirection to the subscription service is important, since many users at subscribing institutions have no idea that they have access to RCM (or that it exists!).  They can stumble onto something on the web and be drawn into it.
[Conversely, the subscription service will refer subscription users who want to use the materials in ways not covered by their educational license to the Hub to obtain spoke services.]

Since many users go to the open web as a first resort, Trove content will be visible, but then we’ll lead them into the richer content and improved discovery that their institution makes available to them.
