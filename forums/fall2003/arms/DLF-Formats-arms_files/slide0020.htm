Sustainability: Transparency
•Degree to which the digital representation is open to direct analysis with basic tools, such as human readability using a text-only editor. 
•Digital formats in which the underlying information is represented simply and directly will be easier to migrate to new formats and more susceptible to digital archaeology; easier development of rendering software for new technical environments.
•Examples:
–Uncompressed raster image bitstream easy to interpret
–Lossy compressed image bitstream requires complex decoding
