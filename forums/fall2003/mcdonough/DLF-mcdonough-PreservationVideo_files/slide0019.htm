Storing 4:4:4 Uncompressed Video
QuickTime – Proprietary,  but publicly documented and does the task, and software support is available MJPEG 2000 – Open Standard (ISO/IEC 15444), supports 4:4:4 uncompressed.  Software support iffy, but growing. MPEG4 – Open Standard, growing industry support, BUT keep a close eye on software support for truly lossless encoding. Material Exchange Format – Open Standard, but software support is weak, and some vendor issues
