Sampling, Migration & Artifacts
4:4:4 sampling fulfills the digital promise of
perfect copies across generations, but
Most video equipment doesn’t actually
support it.  Most high-end video editing
packages on computers do and will store 4:4:4
to disk.
Lesson: if you want to store 4:4:4
uncompressed video, prepare to buy a lot of
disk (or HSM), and abandon videotape.
