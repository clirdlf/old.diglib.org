Mind Games I: Conversion

9 Digitization/Editing workstations: $990,000
9 conversion staff full time for 1 year: $350,000
 425 TB of Disk Storage @ $10k/TB = $4,250,000
Grand Total: $5,590,000
FYI, according to ARL, that’s about half our entire 2002 materials budget
