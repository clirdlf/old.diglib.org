Job Posting – Example 2
•Metadata Librarian Description
–Develops plans to record the descriptive, administrative, and structural metadata needed to support the Libraries’ expanding digital collections.  Advises on the application of appropriate metadata schema for digital objects and assists in defining DLP and other digital project parameters.  Plans for the translation of metadata between formats and participates in the integration of metadata from a variety of sources for search and display within and across collections.
