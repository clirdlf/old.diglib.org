Who is CrossRef?
•CrossRef is a collaborative, non-profit, independent membership organization that employs open standards
•Members: Any publisher of original scholarly material in electronic form
•Libraries: Enrich online catalogues with outgoing links to full text and products they license will have DOIs
•Affiliates and agents: Secondary publishers and journal hosting services are enhancing their products with DOI-based citation links.
CrossRef is a collaborative linking network and the members are primary publishers.  However, libraries and secondary publishers also use the CrossRef system.  We are increasingly collaborating with these organizations.
