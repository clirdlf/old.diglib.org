I am not going to discuss detailed syntax. It is the role of the standardization committee to work that out. However, a few examples should make clear what an OpenURL looks like.
A well-known resolver is SFX. Although OpenURL originated with SFX research, OpenURL will serve many other resolvers besides SFX.
