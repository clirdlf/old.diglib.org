I am not going to discuss detailed syntax. It is the role of the standardization committee to work that out. However, a few examples should make clear what an OpenURL looks like. A well-known resolver is SFX. Although OpenURL originated with SFX research, OpenURL will serve many other resolvers besides SFX.
DOI: a database that links persistent identifiers to non-persistent locations
OpenURL: the service component that is able to take OpenURL as input can be intelligent about the objects and related this information to current location. “This publisher was taken over by that one and this journal currently resides at …”
