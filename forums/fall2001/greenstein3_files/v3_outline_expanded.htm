What needs to be in place in order to…
lower barriers to interoperability - OAI
minimize duplication of digitization and build trust in and use of persistent digital objects - Registry
encourage production of good archival finding aids - EAD workbench
support effective management and dissemination of large and growing heterogeneous digital collections – FEDORA
promote investment in persistent digital archives – work with LC’s NDIIP

What knowledge do we need to assess risk involved in DL investments?
about open source software
about how faculty and students perceive of and use the broader scholarly information landscape - Outsell study

How can we lower risk and build trust in digital collections and services?
Benchmarking digital formats – preservation digital master
Building consensus around metadata schemes – METS, VRA
Building consensus around principles of good practice – IMLS framework
