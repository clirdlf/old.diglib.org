RLG Cultural Materials
What was the initial intent of the project?
Improve access to primary sources
Form an alliance to create a union resource that documents culture and civilization
Increase visibility of collections and institutions
Protect and support institutional rights
Create a framework for targeted digitization initiatives
Help to transform research and learning in the digital environment
Create a reliable, web-based discovery environment
Integrate heterogeneous works and artifacts in all formats, enhancing the usefulness of individual collections
