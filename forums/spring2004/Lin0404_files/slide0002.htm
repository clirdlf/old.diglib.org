ACLS History E-Book Project
• The ACLS History E-Book Project is a collaboration among 10 university presses, 8 learned societies, and the American Council of Learned Societies to publish high quality history books in electronic format.
• Collection currently includes 800 books selected by scholars from  participating learned societies. 250 books are added each year.
•
• Subscription product was launched in September 2002. Access to books via valid IP or proxy server.
•
• Price range $300-$1300 per year.
•
• Project funded by The Andrew W. Mellon Foundation.
