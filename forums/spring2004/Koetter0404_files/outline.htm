What is an emblem?
  • Multi-medial publications of the 17th and 18th centuries
  • Three constitutive elements—a motto, a woodcut or engraving and an explanatory poem
  • More than the sum of its parts
3
"Author - Pfann"
  • Author - Pfann, Johann, fl. 1626-1670
  • Title - Aigentlicher Abriß…
  • Motto (Latin) - Hoc mage castigat DEUS hunc, quo charior ipsi.
  • Motto (German) - So macht's das Herr, das nichts den gut. / Je lieber das Kind, je stärker die Rute. / Seine Züchtigung nicht gering du acht / Und nur allein das Ende betracht.
  • Topos - Control emotions; Spare the rod, spoil the child; Whip as discipline; Untamed horse
  • Theme - Necessity of discipline
  • Descriptors - Horse; Hand from heaven; Cloud; Whip; Landscape, etc.
4
"Author - Sporck"
  • Author - Sporck, Franz Anton, Graf von, 1662-1738
  • Title - Geistliche Todts-Gedancken
  • Motto (German) - Der Bettler
  • Topos - Dance of Death; Heaven and hell
  • Theme - Inevitable death; Reward and punishment
  • Descriptors - man; beggar; bowl; coin; gold; sword; weapon; skeleton; Death; fire; cloud; heaven; hell
5
"Author - Isselburg"
  • Author - Isselburg, Peter, ca. 1568-ca. 1630
  • Title - Emblemata Politica In Aula Magna Curiae Noribergensis Depicta.
  • Motto (Latin) - Sic Orbis iter.
  • Topos - World on the crayfish, crayfish as indirectness
  • Theme - Crookedness of the world
  • Descriptors - lobster; crayfish; orb; globe; world
