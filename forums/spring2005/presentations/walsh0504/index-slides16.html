Conclusion

Technologically the growth of the DLC has been less systematic and disciplined and more organic and free form resulting in an interesting, Frankenstein-like beast put together from a wide variety of technological parts (Perl, PHP, Java, MySQL, Oracle, etc.). This very diverse technological background in many ways reflects the diverse collections and user-base of the Digital Library of the Commons.
