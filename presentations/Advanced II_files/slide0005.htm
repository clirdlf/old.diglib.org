Conclusions
§We have many of the tools and standards we need to advance to much greater deep sharing of metadata – and to get to the next challenges here
§We need closer partnerships with publishers and users to solidify the library’s role at the heart of the new information spaces
§We can see a proliferation of devices and output formats which raises the bar on nimble content
§We can look ahead to richer, deeper, sharing of content, not just metadata about content
§Librarianship skills are central and need to be asserted
