Digitization
oStandards rule: digitizing is expensive enough to do and the results are difficult to preserve, so you better get it mostly right the first time.
o
oStandards rock: archival-quality digital material is nimble, malleable, flexible, and permanent – it can be shaped to users’ evolving needs and therefore much more likely to be successful in the academic arena (donors like to see that what they funded is enduring and successful)
