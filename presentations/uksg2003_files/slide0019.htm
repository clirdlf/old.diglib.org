So many silos, so little time
oWhat too many users do when faced with library lists of “silos we subscribe to”:  they go to Google, for one-stop shopping and to get a result every time; they go to Amazon, often instead of books in print (and even the OPAC); or they go to their “courseware”  homepage (Blackboard, WebCT) . 
o
oThe library is absent from many of the places our users are going, so the licensed material we provide is too often not found. 
o
oWe ignore convenience at our peril.
