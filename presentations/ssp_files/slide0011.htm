An Illustrative problem
nPublishers build websites for journal content.  Libraries license them.  Libraries puts up websites of links to journals to which they subscribe, often arranged alphabetically.
nThe imagined user is:
na) Either someone so specialized that they only need access to one or two journals or sites, and they know what they are.
n b) Someone with so much time that they can go in and out of innumerable data silos and learn multiple interface.
n c) A librarian.
