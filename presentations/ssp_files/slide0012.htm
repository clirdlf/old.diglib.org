An Illustrative problem
nWhat too many users do:  They go to Google, for one-stop shopping and to get a result every time; and they go to their class homepage in Blackboard or WebCT.  The library is absent from most of the places, so the licensed material we provide is too often not found.  “If we build it we will come”.
nA faculty member who is building a reading list on a class web page often takes the paper copy of a journal and scans in the relevant article even if that material is licensed online.  Why?  Because when he or she goes to a site they are so frustrated by the inability to link at a sufficiently granular level that going back to the paper copy is quicker.
