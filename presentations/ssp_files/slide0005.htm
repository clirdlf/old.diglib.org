Archiving & preservation : How long?
nEducational content typically has an academic life much longer than it has a commercial life nUniversity libraries are less ephemeral than publishers over the centuries, and may invest in preservation of content longer than publishers are willing to do so. nSo – as we look forwards and back, let’s look at what the data needs to be (standards for creation and migration) but also at  how it needs to behave (function) in order to maximize its chance of being worthy of archiving.
