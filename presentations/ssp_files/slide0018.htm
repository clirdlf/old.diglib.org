What do we need?
nPartnerships -- We need some commonality of descriptive metadata across publishers and a much greater understanding by publishers of the libraries’ challenges – and vice versa.  We need to find forums in which we can work collaboratively on these shared problems.
nAggregation -- We need the ability to aggregate content from one publisher and journal with content from other publishers and journals. We need the ability to deal with material at an appropriate level of granularity – for a journal, this is the article at the very least.
