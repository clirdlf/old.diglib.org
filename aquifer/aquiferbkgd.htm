PROJECT BACKGROUND

DLF Aquifer project work is distributed amongst four working groups, collections, metadata, technology and services. The Aquifer Implementation Group, coordinated by the DLF Aquifer director, sets policy. Appendix II of the DLF Aquifer Business Plan outlines working group charges and lists members.

Each working group meets regularly by conference call. Both working group members and colleagues in participant libraries engage in project tasks betwen calls.
